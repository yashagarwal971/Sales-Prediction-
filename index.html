Why study tourism?

The World Travel and Tourism Council (WTTC) report found that the travel & tourism industry generated 10.4 percent of all global economic growth. Tourism is not only a kind of leisure, it is also essential in a country's economy. We will analyze and investigate the dynamic trend of evolvement in tourism sector and find the top tourist-sending countries and the countries that are most attractive to interntional tourists.
